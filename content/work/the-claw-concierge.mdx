## Problem

Powerful personal AI is here, but it is intimidating to set up, secure, and actually run. Most people who want the leverage do not want the risk, the config, or the maintenance. There was no trustworthy front door: a brand and a service that says, simply, we will run this for you, done right.

## Build

The Claw Concierge is a managed AI concierge service, shipped as one system: the brand, the marketing site, the booking flow, and the voice agents, all of a piece.

- **Brand and marketing site.** A confident, dark, editorial identity ("Your AI Assistant, Done Right") with the claw mascots, clear plans and pricing, a how-it-works walkthrough, and an FAQ.
- **Booking and lead capture.** A "Book Free Audit" path that turns interest into a scheduled call, wired to the back office.
- **Voice agents.** Voice Staff agents that handle calls around the clock, plus an on-site assistant for instant answers.
- **One system.** The site is not separate from the service. Design, copy, booking, and the agents were built together in a single engagement.

## Outcome

A live service business with a brand that earns trust on first impression, a site that converts browsers into booked calls, and voice agents working 24/7. One operator, one engagement, a working business at the end.

## Stack

Next.js 16 for the site, Vapi for the voice agents, Supabase and Stripe for the back office and payments, Resend for email, Vercel for the deploy.

## What this is, generally

What it looks like when the brand, the site, and the service ship together. Not a logo, then a website, then a tool. One coherent system that a real business can run on from day one.
