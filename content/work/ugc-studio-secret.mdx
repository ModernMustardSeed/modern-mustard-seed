## Problem

Direct-to-consumer brands spend the first two weeks of every campaign on the same loop: research the audience, scout the competition, draft hooks, get them rejected, redraft, storyboard, reject again. A creative agency bills $5,000 to $10,000 a month for that loop and the brand still owns the brief that never quite lands. Most founders skip this work and ship ads that flop, then blame the algorithm.

## Build

UGC Studio Secret is an autonomous creative supply chain. Four specialized AI agents working in sequential chain-of-thought:

**Agent 01: The Scout.** Watches user-uploaded video files to understand pacing and vibe (Gemini 3.0 Pro Vision). Autonomously browses provided URLs with the Google Search Tool to extract pricing, USPs, and brand voice. Outputs raw context data.

**Agent 02: The Strategist.** Identifies the Ideal Customer Avatar and Core Pain Point. Runs a Competitor War Room: analyzes rival URLs to find market gaps. Outputs the Core Angle and a Virality Score.

**Agent 03: The Director.** Writes high-retention scripts with timestamped beats. Defines the exact Creator Archetype (look, feel, acting style). Outputs the full script, shot list, and casting dossier.

**Agent 04: The Simulator.** Uses Gemini 3.0 Pro Image to hallucinate photorealistic previews of every shot. Uses Veo 3.1 to generate 3-second b-roll clips. Uses Gemini 2.5 Flash TTS to perform the script in a specific creator voice. Outputs the Visual Manifest.

The user pastes a product URL or uploads a raw video, picks a competitor or two, and waits 45 seconds. The output is a production-ready ad kit: hook variants, full script with timestamps, storyboard frames, b-roll concepts, casting brief, and a recommended micro-tribe with cold-DM templates for talent recruitment.

## Outcome

Brands move from blank page to production plan in 45 seconds instead of two weeks. Legacy agency cost was $5K to $10K a month for the same deliverable. UGC Studio Secret runs $299 a month and the brand keeps the brief, the scripts, and the visual manifest in their workspace forever.

## Stack

Vite + React + TypeScript on the front end. Google Gemini 3.0 Pro for reasoning, vision, and code. Gemini 3.0 Pro Image (the "Nano Banana" preview model) for storyboards. Veo 3.1 for motion. Gemini 2.5 Flash TTS for voice. Google Search Tool for live web grounding. Firebase Auth + Firestore for users and state. Express for the payment server. Stripe for metered billing. Vercel for the deploy.

## What this is, generally

A working example of an agentic supply chain replacing an expensive recurring human workflow. The pattern is portable. Any creative or analytical process that today moves through four sequential stages (perception, reasoning, synthesis, generation) can be re-built as a four-agent swarm and compressed from weeks to seconds. UGC creative is the first vertical. Real estate underwriting, legal discovery, medical intake, and contractor estimating are the same shape.
