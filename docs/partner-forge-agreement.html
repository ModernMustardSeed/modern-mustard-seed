Partner Demo Agreement

This one page is the whole deal between Modern Mustard Seed ("MMS", Kalispell, Montana) and you, an approved MMS partner, about minting demo suites through the partner forge. Accepting it in the forge records your acceptance with a timestamp.

1. What the forge does

You give us the name, phone, and website of a business you personally know. We build them a free demo suite (an AI receptionist, a demo website, and a business command center demo) presented with your name on it. The demos are marketing samples: they carry the Modern Mustard Seed mark, they are not the business's property, and we take a suite down promptly whenever the business asks.

2. Honesty rules

  • Only mint for businesses you actually know or have a real reason to help. No scraping lists into the forge.
  • Demos are built from the business's own public information. We never invent facts, reviews, or claims.
  • When you hand off a demo, say plainly that MMS built it and that you earn a commission if they buy. The disclosure is the trust.

4. Guardrails

Forge access is granted per partner and capped (your caps show on the forge page). Your first three mints get a human polish pass before hand-off. MMS may decline, edit, or remove any demo, and may revoke forge access at any time; you can walk away at any time. Nothing here makes you an employee or agent of MMS, and neither of us can sign for the other.

5. The boring line

This agreement is governed by Montana law, replaces any earlier chatter about demo minting, and can only be changed by a new version posted in the forge. Everything else about the partner program (codes, payouts, free access) stays as posted on Partner HQ.
