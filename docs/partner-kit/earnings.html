Three worked examples
Example · Product

The Builder Bundle

A $197 product sale, paid at 50% the instant it clears.

$98.50 to you

$197 × 50%. Playbooks run $47 to $67 (about $23 to $34 each), and the Complete Library is $247 (about $123).

Example · Recurring

One AI receptionist

One business you refer onto a Sidekick receptionist at $297/mo, paid at 25% of each invoice.

$74.25 /mo to you

$297 × 25%, every month for the first 12 invoices. That is up to $891 in the first year from one referral, and it keeps paying while you go find the next one.

Example · Build

A custom build

A $3,500 project you bring in, paid at 10% (or 20% once you are a Producer).

$350 to you (up to $700)

$3,500 × 10% at the standard rate. Once you are closing builds regularly, the Producer rate of 20% makes the same project pay $700.

Illustrations only, not income promises. Your earnings depend entirely on what you refer and what those businesses choose to buy, and some may buy nothing. Prices shown are current Modern Mustard Seed prices and can change. Commissions become payable 14 days after the sale (the refund window) and are visible on your Partner HQ ledger at all times.
