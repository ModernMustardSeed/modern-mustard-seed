What the Partner Forge is 02

You bring the trust.
We do the whole build.

Most partner programs hand you a link and wish you luck. The Partner Forge hands you a finished product. You already know business owners who are losing calls, running an ugly website, or drowning in busywork. Now you can hand each of them a real, working demo built for their business, with your name on the front, in about two minutes of your time.

How a mint works
1

Type in a business you know

Their name, a phone number, and their website or Facebook page. That is the whole form. The forge builds only from their real, public information, so you are never inventing anything.

2

A finished suite appears, under your flag

An AI receptionist that answers as their business, a website designed from scratch, and a command center demo. The hub reads "Presented by you, with Modern Mustard Seed." Your referral code is stamped into every checkout on it.

3

Forward three lines

We email you the link and a short, honest hand-off you can send in your own words. They see it real, not as a pitch. If they buy, the commission lands on your ledger automatically. You never touch billing.

What you earn, and the honest limits 03

One structure. The same for everyone.

Our prices are fixed and public, and so is what you earn. No tiers you have to negotiate, no fine print that quietly shrinks your cut.

50%
On every product

Every playbook, bundle, and program pays half the moment someone buys. A $197 bundle pays you about $98. Same rate for everyone.

25%
Recurring, monthly

Put a business on a 24/7 AI receptionist and earn a quarter of every monthly invoice, for the first twelve months. This is the part that compounds.

10–20%
On custom builds

Send a business that needs a real build and earn 10% of the project, moving up to 20% once you are closing them regularly. These are the biggest checks.

The honest guardrails
  • ●Caps, on purpose. New partners start at three mints a day and ten a week. It keeps the forge for businesses you truly know, not scraped lists.
  • ●Your first three mints get a human look. They are held for a quick quality pass before the hand-off email goes out. After three clean ones, that hold lifts.
  • ●First touch wins. If a business is already in our pipeline, the forge will not let you claim it. Great instinct, just pick another one you know.
  • ●Demos are samples, and disclosure is the trust. Every suite carries the Modern Mustard Seed mark, a business can ask us to take theirs down anytime, and you always say plainly that you earn a commission.
