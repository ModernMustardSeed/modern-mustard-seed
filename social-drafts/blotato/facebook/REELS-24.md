# Main Street AI: 24 Facebook Reels, ready to shoot

**How to use this file:** shoot in batches of 8 (about 90 minutes each). Change your shirt between batches so it does not look like one session. Post 4 per week, natively from the Facebook app so you get trending audio and the Reels editor.

**Non-negotiables on every Reel:**
- Hook lands in the first **1.5 seconds**. No "Hey guys." No intro. First words are the surprising thing.
- **On-screen text always.** Most viewers are muted. If the point only exists in audio, it does not exist.
- **20 to 40 seconds.** Watch-through is the ranking input. Cut anything that is not the point.
- **Vertical 9:16.** Shoot at eye level, window light in front of you, phone against something so it does not drift.
- **Link in the FIRST COMMENT, never the caption.** Facebook suppresses body links.
- **Pinned comment on every Reel** points to the group.

**Standard pinned comment (paste on every Reel):**
> Free group where I do this every week: [GROUP LINK]. No pitching, no spam, just the moves that put money back in a small business.

**Shot key:** `FACE` = you talking to camera. `SCREEN` = screen recording with your voice over it. `BOTH` = start on your face, cut to screen, end on your face.

## Shooting notes

**Batch day setup (once, 10 minutes):**
- Phone vertical, propped, lens at eye height. Sitting is fine. A window in front of you, never behind you.
- Wired earbuds or a $20 lav mic. Audio quality matters more than video quality on Facebook.
- Shoot all 8, one take each, in a row. **Do not review between takes**, that's what kills a batch session. Review at the end and reshoot only the ones that are genuinely broken.
- Change shirts between batches so two weeks of posts do not look like one afternoon.

**Editing (5 minutes per Reel, in the Facebook app):**
- Auto-captions on, then fix the words it got wrong. It will get "Sidekick" and business names wrong.
- Add the on-screen text beats at the moments listed. Big, high contrast, upper third or center. Never at the very bottom, the UI covers it.
- Trim dead air at the front. The hook should be the first frame with sound.
- Add a quiet trending audio underneath at low volume if one fits. Do not let it fight your voice.

**Posting:**
- Post natively from the Facebook app, to the Page, as a Reel.
- Caption from this file. **No link in the caption.**
- Immediately drop the pinned comment with the group link.
- Stay on your phone for the next hour and reply to every comment with a question.
- After a day, click the reaction count and invite everyone who reacted to follow the Page.
