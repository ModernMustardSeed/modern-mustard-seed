## Tips for Facebook

- Post at **10:00 AM Mountain** same day as LinkedIn so the feed shows momentum
- First comment trick: drop the URL again in the first comment with "P.S." or "More here:" to boost reach (Facebook deprioritizes posts with outbound links in the body, less so in comments)
- Tag yourself (@Sarah Scarano personal page) on Mon, Thu, and Sat posts. Don't tag every day, looks spammy
- React to your own post within 5 minutes from your personal account to seed engagement
- If a post gets traction (>10 reactions in the first hour), boost it for $5 to extend reach within 24h
