## 1. The One Sentence

A Christian solo founder building real ventures with AI, in public, so other believers can build their own.

If a video does not move that sentence forward, it does not get made.

---

## 2. Positioning

**Who you are on this channel:** the faith-driven AI founder. Not a guru. Not a hype account. A stewardship-minded builder who happens to be unusually good with AI, showing the work honestly.

**The mustard seed spine (Matthew 13:31-32, 17:20):** small faithful inputs, compounded through leverage, become something that shelters others. AI is the leverage. Faith is the soil. That metaphor is the brand and it runs through every pillar.

**Why people subscribe:** they want what you have. Either the skill (build with AI) or the posture (build as a believer without selling your soul to hustle culture). Most want both, and almost nobody is serving both at once. That gap is the channel.

**Who converts and to what:**
- AI-curious founders and operators who need a build → **MMS studio leads** (modernmustardseed.com, $225/hr).
- Solopreneurs who want the system → **digital playbook sales** (MMS store).
- Faith + work audience who resonate with the heart → **CXC apparel** (crossandcovenant.co) and long-term trust.

## 3. Content Pillars (every video is exactly one)

| Pillar | Promise | Primarily sells | Format |
|--------|---------|-----------------|--------|
| **BUILD** | Watch a real feature/venture get built with AI agents | MMS studio | Long-form (screen + voice) |
| **SYSTEMS** | The machinery: how one person runs many ventures | Playbooks | Long-form + Shorts |
| **STEWARD** | Faith + work: calling, stewardship, building as worship | Trust / CXC | Shorts + occasional long-form |
| **STORY** | The journey behind CXC, MMS, the wins and the failures | Connection / all | Long-form + Shorts |

Rotation target (weekly): 1 long-form (BUILD or SYSTEMS or STORY) + 1 supporting long-form OR a STEWARD anchor, plus 5-7 Shorts cut from those and from standalone STEWARD insights.

---

## 4. Voice and Tone

- Warm authority. Conviction without preaching.
- Practical first, spiritual underneath. Show the work, then name the deeper why.
- No hype. No "this will change your life." No fake urgency.
- Stewardship posture: you are managing gifts, not flexing them.
- Speak to one person, a believer who is capable but stuck.
- **No em dashes** in scripts, titles, or descriptions. Periods, commas, parentheses.

Banned: "game-changer," "insane," "secret hack," "passive income while you sleep," anything that would embarrass you in front of your church.

## 5. Visual Language (faceless)

Palette: mustard/gold + ink + warm cream, with one cool accent (aqua). Cinematic, editorial, never stock-photo generic.

Recurring motifs (Higgsfield prompts should pull from these):
- A mustard seed and its growth (macro seed, sprout, tree sheltering birds)
- Hands building (keyboard, sketch, craft, soil)
- Light through windows, golden hour, Montana / Kalispell landscapes (your real home)
- Abstract agent/network imagery for the AI segments (nodes, flowing light, clean not cyberpunk)
- Modern, quiet, sacred-feeling workspaces

Two visual tracks:
1. **Screen track** (BUILD/SYSTEMS): real screen recordings of the actual AI work, the proof.
2. **Cinematic track** (STEWARD/STORY, B-roll, intros/outros): Higgsfield-generated footage in the palette above.

Always: kinetic captions burned in for retention.

## 8. The Funnel (balanced)

Every video carries all three, weighted by pillar:
- **Hook (0-15s):** the promise, no throat-clearing.
- **Value (body):** deliver fully. Earn the ask.
- **Soft CTA (mid):** one natural mention tied to the content.
- **Hard CTA (end):** subscribe + the single best next step for that pillar.

Pillar → primary CTA:
- BUILD/SYSTEMS → "If you want this built for you, the studio link is below."
- STEWARD/STORY → "If this resonated, [CXC piece / free playbook] is below."

One ask per video. Never stack three CTAs.

---

## 9. North-Star Metrics (first 90 days)

- Consistency: ship the weekly long-form + 5 Shorts, every week. Non-negotiable.
- Leading: average view duration > 45% on long-form, Shorts > 80% retention.
- Brand: comments that say "finally someone doing faith + AI honestly."
- Lagging (do not obsess early): subs, then studio inquiries, then sales.

The goal for 90 days is a body of work and a recognizable brand, not viral spikes.
