# Content Calendar — First 30 Days

Goal: 4 long-form videos + ~20 Shorts in month one. Build the spine, prove consistency, seed the funnel. Pillar codes: BUILD, SYSTEMS, STEWARD, STORY.

---

## The first 10 video concepts (your launch slate)

Each has a working title, pillar, hook, the one CTA, and the visual track.

### 1. STORY — "Why a Christian founder is betting everything on AI"
- **Hook:** "I run four companies by myself. People assume that means I never rest. The truth is the opposite, and it starts with one verse."
- **Body:** the mustard seed thesis. Small faithful inputs, AI as leverage, stewardship not hustle. Introduce the four ventures briefly.
- **CTA:** subscribe. (This is the manifesto video, pin it to the channel.)
- **Visual:** cinematic Higgsfield (seed to tree, Montana light, quiet workspace) + a few real screen glimpses.

### 2. BUILD — "I built a full storefront in a weekend with AI agents"
- **Hook:** "This is a real, live ecommerce store. I did not write most of it by hand. Here is exactly how."
- **Body:** walk the CXC or MMS store build, agentic workflow, the actual screen.
- **CTA:** "If you want one built for you, the studio link is below." (MMS lead)
- **Visual:** screen track dominant + cinematic intro/outro.

### 3. SYSTEMS — "The 5 AI systems that run my four companies"
- **Hook:** "One person cannot run four companies. Unless the systems do the running. Here are the five."
- **Body:** memory, agents, content pipeline, lead engine, deploy automation.
- **CTA:** "The playbook for system #2 is free below." (store / lead magnet)
- **Visual:** screen track + kinetic diagrams.

### 4. STEWARD — "Is it a sin to use AI?" (the honest answer)
- **Hook:** "A believer asked me if using AI to build a business is cheating God. I sat with that question for a week."
- **Body:** theology of work, tools, stewardship of talents (Matthew 25). Conviction, grace, nuance.
- **CTA:** "If this spoke to you, [CXC piece] is below." (CXC / trust)
- **Visual:** fully cinematic Higgsfield.

### 5. BUILD — "Watch me turn a prompt into a working app"
- **Hook:** "Zero to a deployed app, real time, no edits to hide the messy parts."
- **Body:** live-ish build of a small tool with Claude Code.
- **CTA:** studio link (MMS lead).
- **Visual:** screen track.

### 6. SYSTEMS — "How I write a week of content in one afternoon"
- **Hook:** "Every video, every post, every email for a week. One afternoon. Here is the machine."
- **Body:** this exact pipeline (meta and powerful), Higgsfield + voice clone + automation.
- **CTA:** "The content system playbook is below." (store)
- **Visual:** screen + cinematic.

### 7. STORY — "The venture that almost broke me"

### 8. STEWARD — "Building as worship, not as idol"

### 9. BUILD — "I gave an AI agent my whole business. Here is what happened."

## Shorts formula (30-60s)

1. First 2 seconds: the single most surprising line, on screen as text.
2. One idea only. No intro, no "hey guys."
3. Burned captions, fast cuts, brand palette.
4. End frame: the seed icon + "@modernmustardseed" + one CTA word ("Subscribe" or "Link below").

## Standalone STEWARD micro-devotional bank (for Shorts)

Quick faith + work hits that need no long-form parent. Each is one verse + one honest reflection + one line about building. Examples to script:
- Matthew 6:33 and building a business that is not your kingdom.
- The parable of the talents and shipping work into the world.
- Rest as resistance to the lie that your output is your worth.
- Proverbs 16:3 and committing a launch to God before the metrics come in.
